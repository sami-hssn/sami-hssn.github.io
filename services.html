What we have to offer

Start Your Fitness Journey Today




Select from our various different personal training services.

Some services are offered virtually with the Legend subscription!*







Weight Training*

Build strength and sculpt your body with our weight training sessions. Tailored to all fitness levels, our expert trainers will guide you through a variety of exercises using free weights, machines, and resistance bands to help you achieve your fitness goals and improve muscle tone.





Cardio*

Elevate your heart rate and burn calories with our high-energy cardio workouts. From running and jumping to dance routines and interval training, our cardio sessions are designed to boost your endurance, increase cardiovascular health, and enhance overall fitness.





Aqua Fitness

Dive into a refreshing workout with aqua fitness. Perfect for all ages and fitness levels, these water-based exercises combine cardio and resistance training to improve flexibility, strength, and cardiovascular health, all while being gentle on the joints.





Cycling*

Pedal your way to fitness with our invigorating cycling classes. Whether you’re looking for a high-intensity workout or a low-impact exercise, our indoor cycling sessions offer a fun and effective way to burn calories, build endurance, and tone your legs.





Yoga*

Find balance and tranquility with our yoga classes. Suitable for all experience levels, our sessions focus on flexibility, strength, and mindfulness through a series of poses and breathing exercises. Enhance your physical and mental well-being in a serene and supportive environment.





Mixed Martial Arts (MMA)

Unleash your inner warrior with our MMA training. Our skilled coaches will teach you techniques from various disciplines, including boxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Muay Thai, providing a comprehensive workout that enhances strength, agility, and self-defense skills.
